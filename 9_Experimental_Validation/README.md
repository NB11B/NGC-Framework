## The Three Gaps

When we began this validation effort, three critical gaps were identified:

### Gap #1: First-Principles Derivation ✓ CLOSED

**Status:** Complete (conceptually)

We have successfully derived the Master Equation from the geometry of SU(2) and octonions, showing that every term has a clear mathematical origin. The derivation demonstrates that the Master Equation is a geometric necessity, not an empirical fit.

**Key achievement:** The factor of 7 comes from the 7 imaginary units of the octonions (2³ - 1), connecting the Master Equation to the Cayley-Dickson construction and exceptional Lie groups.

### Gap #2: Falsifiable Experiment ✓ DESIGNED

**Status:** Protocol complete, ready to execute

We have designed a rigorous experimental protocol to test whether asymmetry is necessary for spin computation. The experiment uses GeoFlow with controlled variations in learning rate configuration and measures coherence, stability, and phase transition detection.

**Timeline:** 1 week to execute and analyze

### Gap #3: Quantum Connection ⚠ OPEN

**Status:** Requires collaboration with quantum theorists

The remaining gap is to formally establish the connection between this classical spin computation and quantum mechanical spin. This requires showing that the dual-field system is a coarse-grained version of quantum spin and deriving the relationship between Δ (classical asymmetry) and quantum spin operators.

**Timeline:** 6-12 months with expert collaboration
